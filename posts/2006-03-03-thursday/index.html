Thursday

I went out last night and talked to a relative stranger for about 4 hours. Was good. I’m sure the wine helped :-) Actually, it was probably the good social skills of my poor victim. I usually struggle to make small-talk – I shouldn’t really be belittling the conversation I was having by calling it small talk. It wasn’t small. It was talk, chat, get to know you kind of stuff. I really enjoyed myself. Might do that again.
