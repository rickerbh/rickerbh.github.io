Got me some new D&G's

I got one of the girls I work with to come out sunglasses shopping with me last week. Went down to a parallel importer on queen street, and they didn’t have anything I was interested in. I wanted some more fashionable glasses, not sports styled glasses. I already have some adidas ones, but I felt I needed a change.

So, into Sunglass Hut on Queen Street, have a look around, and I get very confused. I found it very difficult to determine which glasses are for which sex – there are some obvious female-themed glasses, but the mens ones are more difficult to distinguish. So, I speak to the clerk, and she gets me out quite a few pairs.

Long story short, the first pair I try on are the best. D&G 2075’s.

Nice.
