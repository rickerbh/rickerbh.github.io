Not New But….

Mar 29th, 2006 10:36 am | Comments

…It’s new to me. Given the recent hype because of the movie, and the fact that a couple of my friends are really into him, I thought I should check out Johnny Cash. In my usual style, I’ve gone way overboard. I’ve got hold of the following albums. American 3 American 4 American Recordings At Folsom Prison At San Quentin Love, God, Murder [2cd] Man In Black (54-58) [5cd] Man In Black (59-62) [5cd] Unchained Unearthed [5cd] So, I’m going to be all cashed out at the end of this I suspect. I’ve listened to American 3 so far. I give it a 3/5. One and Won’t Back Down are the best tracks there IMHO.
